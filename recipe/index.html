Quinoa Chili

A delicious, healthy, and easy to make dish!

*Vegan *Vegetarian *Gluten Free

Cook time: 40 min
Serves: 6 people


Ingredients

  • 1 can black beans (rinsed)
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 medium onion or leak
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 2/3 cup quinoa
  • 2 tbls extra virgin olive oil
  • 3/4 tbls salt
  • 3/4 tsp chili powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon

Directions

    Step 1:

    Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat.

    Step 2:

    Add garlic, onion, red pepper.

    Step 3:

    Saute until the vegetables just begin to soften.

    Step 4:

    Pour in:

    • Tomato sauce plus 1 can of water
    • Quinoa
    • Corn
    • Beans
    • Salt
    • Chili powder
    • Cumin
    • Cinnamon

    Step 5:

    Stir.

    Step 6:

    Simmer 30 minutes, stirring frequently.

    Step 7:

    Serve and ENJOY!
